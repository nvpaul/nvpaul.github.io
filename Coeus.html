Coeus is a voice skill designed for the Amazon Alexa platform. The skill aims to education users about health and wellness through gamification. Specifically, Coeus is a multiple choice quiz that asks the user a total of three questions. After guessing, Coeus provides the user with the correct answer and either a related fact or more detailed information.

Follow along with my design thinking process to learn more about what Coeus is, why Coeus was created, and how Coeus was developed.

Status: Proof of Concept, Fall 2020

Define

The life expectancy (LE) and health-adjusted life expectancy (HALE) in Canada have been increasing consistently over the past decade, however, two-thirds of Canadian adults live with at least one modifiable risk factor for chronic disease, including:

  • Tobacco smoking
  • Physical inactivity
  • Sedentary behaviour
  • Unhealthy eating
  • Harmful use of alcohol
  • Obesity

There are many tools and resources on the existing web and mobile platforms designed to help individuals live healthier lives, however, the new voice platform (Amazon Alexa and Google Home) are increasing in popularity, especially among younger generations, and have the potential to deliver information regarding health and wellness to users hands-free, easily fitting into their busy schedules.

Some of Canada's stats regarding use of the voice platform are as follows:
26% of Canadians are using the voice platform as of 2020.
1/5 Canadian internet users use smart speakers at least once a month as of 2019, with an expected growth of 15% by 2020.

With the voice platform becoming increasingly popular among Canadian's, it is increasingly common to use voice searches to convey information to users. We could use a voice skill to help teach Canadians about health and wellness and encourage healthy lifestyles, hopefully preventing users from falling into the categories of modifiable risk factors listed above.

Ideate

Idea 1:
Voice Habit Tracker

How it Works
Similar to a habit tracking app that can be found on mobile, the skill would asks users to sign on every day to record the status of their selected habits, for example drinking water or going for a walk.

Pros

  • Allows users to track habits hands-free, without requiring a mobile device

Cons

  • Unlike mobile devices which send notifications, the voice platform requires users to remember themselves

Idea 2:
Daily Briefing Health Tip

How it Works
This voice skill on Amazon Alexa would use the daily briefing feature to provide users with a health tip or fact.

Pros

  • Provides relevant information to users hands-free

Cons

  • The fact must be short and simple to keep users attention
  • Ensure the fact is randomized among a large dataset, preventing repeated information

Idea 3:
Gamified Health Quiz

How it Works
Using the theory of gamification to encourage use of the skill, this skill would ask the user a series of multiple-choice questions, and provide the user with a related fact within every answer.

Pros

  • Provide users with a simple fact or tips related to each question
  • Gamify the experience

Cons

  • A gamified quiz is more challenging to use for users who struggle with technology
